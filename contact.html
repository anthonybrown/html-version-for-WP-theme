Plan

Once all the information is gathered, it is processed and a plan if formed. This is the first step in the strategic process that I use.

This stage sets up a direction for the project to follow. Once a budget and timeline are formed, the technologies needed to accomplish the goals are weighed out over the timeline and budget.

I ask questions, gather your ideas, learn your business objectives and wrap my mind around what needs to be done to accomplish your goals

design

With the plan established, design and concept can begin to take place. The design process consists of some initial sketches to make sure we are communicated well. Then I start with the wireframe of what the application will look like.

Through this process, the idea is shown and appropriate changes are made while in the initial phase, there is no need for a major revisions.

Through constant refinement of the initial sketch, I am able to narrow down the design to the final solution

build

Once the design has gone through the process and the final draft is approved, the meticulous process of slicing and coding your project can begin.

The appropriate technologies will be used, I hand craft all the code and use semantic standards based practices that are industries highest standards. Pixel perfect placement and cross browser compatibility are tested and refined.

We test and test and then test some more until we are sure that your site gets the seal of approval.

launch

Houston we have a go !

Once we are satisfied that all is well and your site performs in all the major browsers and plateforms, we lanch the site in a live environment.

Once the project is live, I will continue to be in touch to make sure that the application or site is working on the live server. Support is as long or little as you want and your site will be burned on a CD for your personal backup incash your server melts or something bad happens, you will be covered
