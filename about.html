A Little Bit About Me

Hi my name is Tony and I am obsessed with web technologies, typography, design & development of all things web. The web has given me an opportunity to grow as a person,and an artist. How does this relate to the web, with the web I can explore my art with photoshop and illustrator. I can see my idea's come to life and it is fun for me to make this happen. I use all my God given talents to produce wonderful graphics and beautiful code, it all goes hand in hand. In life we have very little control of what happens, but here I can control things on a pixel level playing field, I like that, I also like helping people get their message or business across to as many people as possible.

I am currently finishing my Bachelor's degree in web design and interactive media at the New England Institute of Art in Boston. The web has given me a place where I can bring all my talents into a environment that I can be a creative. Slicing Photoshop files and writing semantic markup with some modern css and a sprinkling of JavaScript and some PHP and mySQL for good measure and of course Wordpress are my passions, and I am lucky to be working with stuff that I am passionate about.

At Ai, my professors taught me the importance of usability, information architecture and interface design, as well as how to code, the Language is not as important as understanding the logic behind what you are writing, it's about your audience and their experience using your application or site. They taught me the finer details of the business, how to establish a working relationship with my clients and how to really know what I am talking about

At the moment I am freelancing, I provide clients with handcrafted web sites, logo's and company branding. I suggest a responsive design which will enable the sites to adjust the style for viewport of the device, translation: your site will look and function great regardless of what your audience is viewing it on.I can offer e-commerce, social media sites, membership sites, blogs, what ever you need.

At the moment I am available for work
